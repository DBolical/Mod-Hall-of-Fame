Welcome to the Mod Hall of Fame, a celebration of the best video game modifications ever made over the past 25 years. Every mod on this list has earned its spot be it via commercial or critical acclaim and has influenced gaming in some way. Millions of mods have been released since the earliest days of video gaming, so whilst we cannot list them all, we attempted to pick the standout mods spanning various genres and games. Each year we shall consider new and old mods for inclusion on this list, enjoy the best of the best!
